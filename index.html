Two shores, one current

Born on the Atlantic. Engineered in the North Sea.

brxpower grew out of a simple observation: Brazil has the wind and the sun, and Denmark has spent forty years learning how to build with them. We sit between the two — Rio de Janeiro and Copenhagen — pairing local roots with the engineering culture that made Nordic renewables the standard.

That means projects developed with local communities and grid realities in mind, held to the diligence, transparency and long-horizon thinking that institutional partners expect.
